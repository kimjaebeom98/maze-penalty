About Maze Penalty

Making Group Decisions Fun

The exciting alternative to boring ladder games

Our Story

Maze Penalty was born from a simple idea: group decisions don't have to be boring. Traditional methods like ladder games or coin flips work, but they lack excitement and engagement. We wanted to create something that turns every decision into a memorable moment.

Whether you're deciding who pays for dinner, who has to do a dare, or who picks the next movie, Maze Penalty adds suspense, strategy (through power-ups), and plenty of laughs to the process.

What Makes Us Different

Interactive Gameplay

Watch the race unfold in real-time with smooth animations and exciting power-ups that can change everything at the last moment.

Fair Randomness

Our algorithm ensures every player has a fair chance. Random item placements and maze generation keep every game unique.

Group-Friendly

Designed for 2-8 players, perfect for any gathering from small get-togethers to large parties.

Accessible Everywhere

No downloads, no accounts, no hassle. Just open the website and start playing on any device.

Perfect For

  • Deciding who pays for meals
  • Party games and icebreakers
  • Drinking game penalties
  • Random selection for any occasion
  • Team building activities
  • Family game nights
